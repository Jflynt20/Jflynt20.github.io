First post

Posted on February 26, 2019

The Simpson County Academy high school basketball team was hosting the 4A state tournament. In which they were playing against the Copiah colonels for the state title. .

Led by 6 seniors, 2 juniors,and a sophmore point guard, the cougars jumped to an early lead. Although early foul trouble kept the cougars from being able to pull away throughout the game. They still managed o secure their lead in the second half with great free throw shooting from their players. The Colonels could never get within striking distance. This win meant a lot for the school and the team, but the coolest part was this was their coaches overall 1000th win as a high school basketball coach so it made the night that much better.
